Welcome to Kollam

Kollam is a beautiful town in Kerala, India.

Kollam is a green and peaceful district in Kerala. It has backwaters, beaches, temples, and coconut trees. It is also known for cashew processing and old trading history. The beautiful Ashtamudi Lake is very famous here.

Places to Visit

  • Ashtamudi Lake
  • Thangassery Light House
  • Thenmala Eco Tourism
  • Palaruvi Waterfalls

Come visit Kollam and enjoy nature!
