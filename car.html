Tesla

Model S

"To accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy." ~ Elon Musk

The Tesla Model S is an all-electric luxury sedan made by Tesla, Inc. It is known for its long range, fast acceleration, and advanced technology.

The Model S can go from 0 to 60 mph in just about 2 seconds, making it one of the fastest electric cars in the world. It has a sleek design and a big touchscreen inside.
